Edutic - Educode - education and technology in the classroom, Brussels, Belgium

An international conference in the center of Brussels, Sunday 19 August - Wednesday 22 August 2018

During 4 days, in Bozar and the Royal Academy, teachers, school directors, parents and students, researchers are invited to reflect on the use of digital technology in teaching and teaching through digital all levels (primary, secondary and higher), with a part devoted to programming.

The event will be organized in 4 days for 4 audiences:

  1. 1 open days for the general public (Sunday 19 August and may be extended to Tuesday 21 August if there are strong expressions of interest)
  2. 2 days dedicated to teachers (Monday, August 20 and Tuesday, August 21)
  3. 1 day dedicated to school directors (Tuesday, August 21) 
  4. 1 day devoted to scientific research on pedagogy, teaching and digital technologies (Wednesday 22 August)

Contents

Some lectures but mainly practical workshops and presentations, especially by teachers and their students, or by representatives of various specialized companies or associations that contribute to the training in the use of ICT at school (such as KodoWallonia, Devoxx4kids, SiCarré©, The hand in the dough, the Scientothèque¨que, the experimentarium, Molengeek, the actors of Fablab ...)

The steering team of the conference wishes to put at the service of the Pact of Excellence its knowledge of the actors and its capacity of organization of meetings between professionals of various horizons.
